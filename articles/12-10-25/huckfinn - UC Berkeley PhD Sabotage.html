By MARGARET SULLIVAN-PRICE | HuckFinn National Affairs Desk

Jiarui Zou, 26, a doctoral candidate in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences‚Äîthe literal science of making computers work‚Äîhas been charged with three felony counts of vandalism after allegedly making computers do the opposite of working. The damage totaled $46,855 over multiple years, during which time UC Berkeley‚Äîhome to seven Nobel laureates and the birthplace of UNIX‚Äîproved institutionally unable to identify a pattern that a Target loss prevention associate would flag in a single shift.
